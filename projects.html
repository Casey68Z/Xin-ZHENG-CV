Projects

Research on Digital Inclusion of Chinese Technology Enterprises | NGO Green Lights Year (2022-2023)

  • Led a 30-person team of undergraduates to complete an independent report.
  • Investigated the digital inclusion status of Chinese technology companies through desktop research, literature reviews, and corporate interviews, and explored practices and potential regulatory policies to promote digital inclusion.
  • Produced the first systematic research report on digital inclusion performance and cutting-edge methods of Chinese technology companies, which was published in both Chinese and English and announced at COP27 in collaboration with the World Benchmark Alliance.

Research on Development of Listed Corporations in Advanced Areas | Think Tank of University (2021)

  • Collected, organized, counted, and analyzed the data and information of listed companies in developed regions of China through the Wind database using SPSS for quantitative data analysis.
  • Studied the relationship between the development status of listed companies in the four advanced economic development regions of Shanghai, Beijing, Shenzhen, and Hangzhou and the government’s policy support for listed companies; analyzed and obtained policy advice and helped bolster Suzhou’s development of enterprises in Suzhou Industrial Park.
  • Independently wrote five research reports and co-wrote a political paper of over 100,000 words on high-quality company development policy recommendations for the Suzhou Industrial Park government.
